Frank Wang

Sr. Broker Manager

When it comes to your mortgage, it is important to make sure you get the home you really want, with flexible financing solutions that are right for you. I’m here to help you get the mortgage you need to suit your lifestyle and to make your home ownership goals happen.

Supported by the considerable resources and expertise of Verico Elite Choice Mortgage Group, I will provide you expert advice and service for your home financing needs. We are licensed Mortgage Professionals who have been operating for over ten years. You’ll enjoy the confidence of knowing you are working with a professional who has only your best interests in mind. From city condos to country acreages to commercial properties, I take on each mortgage as a personal mission from pre-approval to possession, often times going above and beyond the call of duty for my clients.
